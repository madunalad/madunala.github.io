About

The Engineer
Behind the Data.

I'm a data engineer with just over five years of experience, mostly in healthcare and enterprise data platforms. My day-to-day has been building the kind of pipelines that people don't notice until they break — high-volume GCP and AWS architectures, real-time streaming systems, and lately, the data infrastructure that makes Gen AI products actually work in production.

Over the past year at Trinity Health, I've shifted a meaningful chunk of my work toward AI-readiness — building RAG pipelines, wiring up vector databases, and getting ML models out of notebooks and into reliable production systems with proper versioning and monitoring.

Some things I'm proud of: cutting clinical reporting latency from 4 hours to 15 minutes, saving my team $20K+ in cloud spend, and building a document retrieval system that genuinely changed how clinical staff search patient records. I care about data quality, clean architecture, and making sure what I build is something others can actually maintain.
